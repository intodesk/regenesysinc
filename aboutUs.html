About Us

The team at Regenesys Inc. has over 30 years of experience and expertise in filtration and purification systems. Regenesys team has a track record in the development of technologies and systems to handle liquid waste resources efficiently and responsibly, the key factor to contribute towards the global environment. From the initial stage of an inquiry down to the final stage of shipping a finished system, Regenesys's team of sales representatives, engineers, manufacturing team and suppliers ensure that Regenesys client receives a quality product and customer support.

Regenesys offers cost effective solutions through providing a one stop shop service which incorporates designing, manufacturing and distribution of Industrial Oil Filtration Equipment for the Power Generation, Transmission & Distribution industries, as well as other markets which include Oil Reclamation Service Companies and Oil Refineries.
